FAQs

1. Ambulance Service

  • Q: How can I book an ambulance for an emergency?
    A: You can book an ambulance by calling our 24/7 helpline or through our website/mobile app. Simply select your location, and an ambulance will be dispatched to you immediately.
  • Q: What types of ambulances are available?
    A: We offer various types of ambulances, including Basic Life Support (BLS), Advanced Life Support (ALS), and Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) ambulances, depending on your needs.
  • Q: Are your ambulances equipped with necessary medical equipment?
    A: Yes, all our ambulances are fully equipped with essential medical equipment and staffed with trained paramedics to handle emergencies.

2. Labor Management

  • Q: How do you ensure proper workforce allocation?
    A: Our labor management system ensures that the right number of workers with appropriate skills are allocated to each project, optimizing productivity and minimizing delays.
  • Q: Can you help with compliance and safety regulations?
    A: Yes, we manage compliance with all local labor laws and safety regulations to ensure a safe and legally compliant working environment.
  • Q: Do you provide training for the workforce?
    A: Yes, we provide training programs to upskill workers and ensure they are well-prepared for their roles.

3. Biomass Briquettes

  • Q: What are biomass briquettes?
    A: Biomass briquettes are eco-friendly fuel alternatives made from agricultural waste, such as sawdust, rice husks, and other biomass materials. They are used as a substitute for coal, charcoal, and firewood.
  • Q: What are the advantages of using biomass briquettes?
    A: Biomass briquettes are renewable, reduce pollution, have a high calorific value, and are cost-effective compared to conventional fuels like coal and firewood.
  • Q: How can I order biomass briquettes?
    A: You can place an order for biomass briquettes through our website, by contacting our sales team, or via our mobile app. Delivery options are available.
